# AutoResearch Chunk Manifests

This directory contains metadata-only chunk manifests for the local AutoResearch workflow.

The manifests include chunk identifiers, paper metadata, section labels, chunk sizes, chunker metadata, and SHA256 hashes of local chunk text. They intentionally do not include paper text, PDF files, TEI XML, full paper content, or local filesystem paths.

These records are provenance/index artifacts only. They are not manuscript evidence by themselves and do not replace human review of the underlying papers.
